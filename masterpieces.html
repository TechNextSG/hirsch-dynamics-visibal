Packaging Solutions

Packaging Types

Shelf-ready, sterile, or premium retail — manufactured and assembled under one roof, to spec.

Rigid Box

Premium rigid packaging for high-end dental products and OEM brand presentation. Custom sizes, finishes, and print runs from 500 units.

Multi-Format System

Modular packaging accommodating multiple product variants in a single configurable format — ideal for bracket or aligner kit assemblies.

Inner Tray

Thermoformed or injection-molded inner trays for secure component positioning. Custom-fit to product geometry — protects in transit, presents on unboxing.

Single Unit Pack

Individual unit packaging for clinical-grade dental components. Peel-and-use format compliant with sterility requirements and distribution standards.

Sterile Packaging

ISO 11607-compliant sterile barrier systems for Class I & II medical devices. Compatible with EO, gamma, and e-beam sterilisation — Tyvek®, pouches, and lidding.

Private Label & Print

Full custom print, embossing, foiling, and labelling under your brand. Multilingual inserts, regulatory labelling, and UDI support available.
